Let's spend 30 minutes discovering a new idea! Schedule a time and we can chat about making excellent internet or anything at all.

I recently enjoyed 10 years with The Walt Disney Company as technical leader and Staff Solutions Architect. I worked closely with Enterprise Architecture and Security teams to set standards and provide services to a global workforce. A few favorite projects include:

  • A custom employee directory (~1 million records from multiple HCMs including Workday and SAP)
  • Custom knowledge management systems ( e.g. reference cloud architectures, theme park employee resources, legal brand guidelines, Disney archives, infosec standards)
  • Consulting on identity and access management systems from on-prem CA SiteMinder (2014) to Okta and SailPoint (2024) integrating SAML, OAuth, AWS Cognito, etc.
